About DianaMik

Structured Healthcare Consultancy Built on Compliance & Clarity

DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy, now DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP, was founded on 16th August 2018 by Dr. Hardik V Barot, a qualified hospital management professional.

After completing his studies in 2016, Dr. Barot embarked on a career dedicated to enhancing healthcare quality and administrative practices. He served as the Chief Hospital Administrator at SAVA Multispeciality Hospital, Surendranagar (Gujarat), focusing on accreditation, planning, and quality excellence. His leadership contributed to the hospital's recognition as a top healthcare facility in the region, with a 4.3-star rating based on patient reviews.

Throughout his career, Dr. Barot has collaborated with over 200 hospitals as a consultant for accreditation, new hospital planning and design, quality assurance, legal compliance, cashless empanelment, healthcare IT, and infrastructure consulting in accordance with relevant regulations.

DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP has an extensive track record of successfully completing over 150 hospitals NABH accreditation & certification projects, over 25 hospital planning and design projects, and facilitating more than 50 hospitals with TPA & Health Insurance Companies empanelment. Additionally, the team is adept at securing government scheme tie-ups for 10 hospitals and achieving Silver certification for 5 others.

We are dedicated to transforming hospitals and healthcare facilities into models of efficiency, compassion, and innovation. With a profound understanding of the complexities inherent in hospital operations, we offer tailored solutions that bridge the gap between clinical excellence and administrative effectiveness.
